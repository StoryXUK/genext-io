The Vision that drives our Team

GeNext isn’t just a certification—it’s a movement. We exist to bridge the gap between what truly matters in business and what gets recognised. We believe small businesses are the backbone of thriving communities, yet too often go unnoticed by traditional awards and frameworks. That’s why we created GeNext: an accessible, values-based certification designed to celebrate what makes local businesses special.

  • Better Business Through Better Values

    At the heart of GeNext is a simple mission: to help small businesses be recognised for doing right by their customers, their people, and their communities.

Built With You, For You

Our framework isn’t built in a boardroom—it’s co-created with small businesses across the UK. Everything we do is shaped by their feedback, needs, and ideas. That’s how we ensure GeNext remains relevant, real, and rewarding.

  • Co-Creation with Businesses

    We collaborate directly with business owners to develop and refine our certification criteria.

  • Accessibility at its Core

    GeNext is intentionally affordable, straightforward, and achievable for businesses of every size and shape.
